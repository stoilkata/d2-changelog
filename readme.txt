NECROMANCER
- Corpse explosion: damage rescaled. Min damage = corpse explosion level / 2. Max damage = corpse explosion level. Damage is 90% fire instead of 50/50 fire/physical.
- Life tap: life stolen reduced from 50% to 15%.
- Iron maiden replaced with new skill "Amplify Magic" (magic version of Amplify Damage).
- Raise skeletal mage: mages now shoot Bone Spear. Damage is improved by synergies. 
- Skeletons: both skeleton warrior and skeleton mage have slightly increased health, defense, resistance, damage, and block.
- Bone Spear: shoots multiple projectiles, increasing by 1 for every 20 levels (soft points).
- Poison Dagger replaced with new skill "Poison Orb": like Frozen Orb, but Poison.
- Poison Explosion replaced with new skill "Pestilence": Buff that deals poison damage to nearby enemies like an aura.

ASSASSIN
- Death sentry: corpse explosion damage reduced from 40-80 to 10-20 and damage is 90% fire instead of 50/50 physical/fire. Base radius reduced from 10 to 5 (still scales 1/level) (1 = 0.3y). Shots fired increased from 5 to 8.
- Cloak of shadows: no longer considered a curse, so it doesn't override other curses.
- Dragon Tail: radius increased 
- Charge up skills don't lose charges on release, but duration reduced from 15 to 5 seconds.
- Blade Sentinel replaced with new skill "Blade Storm": throws blades in all directions. Can proc on striking effects.
- Blade Fury replcaed with new skill "Blade Toss": Throws multiple blades forward, max projectiles 7. Can proc on striking effects and doesn't require standing still to cast.
- Blade Storm and Blade Toss synergize with each other, Blade Shield synergy removed.

BARBARIAN
- War Cry (reworked): initial cooldown 5.2s. Stun length per level reduced from 5 frames (0.2s) to 2 frames (0.08s). Flat damage rescaled and 25% weapon damage added (now can proc life tap and steal life). Synergies reworked: now gets synergy from shout (2 frames cooldown reduced per level (0.8s)), battle orders (2 frames cooldown reduced per level (0.8s)), and cleave (10% damage per level).
- Concentrate replaced with new skill "Cleave": deals frontal cone splash damage based on weapon damage (100%). Initial cooldown is 1.6s and each level lowers the cooldown until it has no cooldown at level 20 (hard points).
- Battle Cry and Taunt are no longer considered curses, so they don't override other curses.

DRUID
- Fire claws (reworked): deals frontal cone splash fire damage. Synergy Molten Boulder changed to Armageddon.
- Hunger replaced with new skill "Glacial Fangs": deals frontal cone splash cold damage. Receives bonuses from "Tornado", "Twister", "Hurricane"
- Fissure - can be cast in werewolf/werebear form.
- Summon spirit wolf: number of summons rescaled to 5 + 1 per 20 points.
- Summon dire wolf: number of summons rescaled to 3 + 1 per 20 points.
- Summon grizzly: number of summons rescaled to 1 + 1 per 20 points.
- Grizzly/spirit wolf/dire wolf stats nerfed.
- Primal Leap (teleport that works only in werebear/werewolf form) - can only be acquired via items. Teleport on a 2 second cooldown.
- Twister replaced with new skill "Avalanche": blizzard-type skill that does physical damage
- Tornado: shoots multiple projectiles, increasing by 1 for every 20 levels (soft points).

SORCERESS 
- Thunderstorm - releases Nova missiles on impact

CUBE RECIPES
- Annihilus with teleport: now needs only 1 zod rune instead of 3.
- Added corruptions: item + orb of corruption (currently there are some generic bonuses that are the same for all items. This is a work in progress so it will be changed as the season progresses)
- Ist + Herb (drops from Blood Raven Hell) + Helm = Helm with +1 to Primal Leap

MERCENARIES
- Act 5 combat: uses the new skill Cleave instead of Frenzy.

ITEMS

- Added fire/cold/poison version of Griffon
- Added upgradable charm - the base charm drops from Elders (currently upgrades are not implemented, but feel free to find the base charm. Will be announced later on.)

GENERAL

- 7 new maps - each map is opened (only on act5 hell!) with a scroll which drops from Andariel/Duriel/Mephisto/Diablo/Baal/Izual/Hephasto in Hell. New Unique items drop here and everything else, including torch and annihilus
- Added physical and magic pierce
- Elders moved to Act1 Hole Level 1
- Some areas are reverted back to their original level values and only slightly increased density
